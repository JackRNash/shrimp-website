The Whistling Shrimp

Est. 1984

The Whistling Shrimp (est. 1984) is Cornell's oldest comedy troupe. We do long form improvisational comedy. We perform two big shows a semester, with various gigs, mini shows and festivals speckled in between. If you have any questions, inquiries, curiosities or comments, don't hesitate to email us at shrimp@cornell.edu.
